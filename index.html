About Me

I'm Khyathi Kiran Pediredla, an Electrical Engineering undergraduate at IIT (BHU) Varanasi (2024–2028). I love learning by building — taking an idea from a rough sketch all the way to a deployed, working product.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of AI and the web: a markerless fitness digital-twin that estimates fatigue in real time, a legal-research co-pilot for Indian advocates, and an AI image-styling app. I care about clean, responsive interfaces and code that's easy to read.

Comfortable across the stack with TypeScript, React/Next.js and Node.js, I'm always shipping side projects and picking up something new along the way.
